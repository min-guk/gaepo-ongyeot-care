# 온이 Brand Usage

## Character

온이는 a calm apricot-gold neighborhood bird wearing a sage leaf scarf. The round body signals warmth; the leaf signals recovery and grounded guidance; the dotted path suggests finding the next step without haste.

The code-native source is `src/components/brand/oni-mascot.tsx`. It has no external image, font, script, filter, or asset dependency.

## Accessibility

- Use `decorative` when nearby text already communicates the idea. The SVG then receives `aria-hidden="true"`.
- Use the default informative variant only when the illustration deserves a short standalone description. It receives `role="img"`, an accessible label, and a title.
- Never use 온이 as the only source of an instruction, state, error, or link meaning.
- Do not place text inside the SVG. Korean copy must remain selectable, zoomable HTML.
- At small sizes, use the head/body silhouette without relying on the eye, scarf folds, or facial detail to communicate meaning.

## Visual rules

- Body: apricot `#E99B5A`; light: gold `#EDB75F`; scarf: sage `#91AA83`; outline: ink `#243029`.
- Keep strong ink outlines and a light background. Avoid placing the full-color mascot over photographs or busy textures.
- Gentle wing rotation and a single page-entry transition are enough. Reduced-motion preferences collapse all animation.
- Do not infantilize 온이 with baby-talk, exaggerated tears, medical costumes, or gamified care states.

`개포 온곁 재가복지센터` is a working brand and must display `(가칭)` until legal name, domain, and trademark checks are complete. The mascot does not imply designation, licensing, years of operation, or service availability.
