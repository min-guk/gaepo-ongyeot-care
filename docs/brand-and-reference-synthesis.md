## Reference synthesis (patterns, not copied expression)

### Caring — <https://caring.co.kr/visit>

Observed patterns:
- Opens with the problem of not knowing how to start and brings long-term-care grade guidance close to the first screen.
- Groups visit-care tasks into recognizable everyday categories.
- Repeats consultation access after dense explanations and finishes with practical FAQs.

Adapted without copying:
- The home and process pages begin from the caregiver's uncertainty, not a provider boast.
- General help categories use everyday scenes, and a clear disclaimer separates education from this center's unverified designated services.
- A persistent consultation panel remains visible across core pages, but unresolved contact facts render only as disabled status text.

Explicitly not adopted:
- Statistics, awards, testimonials, subsidy percentages, calculator figures, response-time or nationwide claims.

### Visiting Angels — <https://www.visitingangels.com/homecare-services>

Observed patterns:
- Organizes care by needs and recognizable service categories.
- Makes local availability part of the decision path and repeatedly notes that offerings can vary by location.
- Pairs FAQs with educational resource paths.

Adapted without copying:
- Services are described by the visitor's daily situation rather than internal program terminology.
- Verified service-area and designated-service facts have a dedicated fail-closed status block.
- Each decision-heavy page offers a low-pressure next reading path.

Explicitly not adopted:
- Franchise claims, named specialized clinical services, availability, screening credentials, or service guarantees.

### Home Instead — <https://www.homeinstead.com/home-care-services/>

Observed patterns:
- Starts from persona and urgency: who needs care, what kind, how soon, and where.
- Explains the process with short numbered steps in plain language.
- Offers a no-pressure resource/consultation path for people still planning or unsure.

Adapted without copying:
- Home gives three persona-led entry paths for family worry, unfamiliar services, and job interest.
- Process/costs uses a five-step preparation sequence and a cost-question checklist without publishing unverified numbers.
- Copy repeatedly permits visitors to read, compare, and delay a decision.

Explicitly not adopted:
- Ratings, testimonials, quick-start timing, free-consultation promises, always-available claims, or local price language.

## Information and claim rules

1. General educational taxonomy is always labeled as general, not as a promise of provision.
2. Any actual service, area, address, hours, phone, Kakao, designation, privacy approval, or cost number comes only through verified facts or future source-reviewed content.
3. Contact and recruitment pages remain informational in G003; G005 owns live forms and integrations.
4. There are no invented testimonials, ratings, awards, years-in-business, staff counts, response times, or outcomes.
5. The site adds no auth, CMS, database, booking, payment, portal, care record, or file upload.
